Tadig Resturant

Tadig is a persian resturant with a warm and friendly environment located in heart of Stockholm. Introducing the persian signature dishes to the world and providing the best quality foods, with the best service is our top goal. Tadig is scorched rice, also known as crunchy rice, is a thin crust of slightly browned rice at the bottom of the cooking pot. It is produced during the cooking of rice over direct heat from a flame. As it's a specialty of Iranian cuisine and it is traditionally served to guests at a meal, all our main dishes are also provided with a slice of Tadig served with your dish. Thank you for choosing us.

In case of any food allergies or special diet please inform our staff before ordering your meal. We also provide a simple guide through out the menu to help you choose your favorite dish. Enjoy your meal.
